ERP Selection

Choose ERP Based on Requirements, Not Vendor Pressure

Midwest ERP helps leadership teams define requirements, compare platforms, score vendors, evaluate implementation partners, and select the ERP path that fits the business.

Start Selection Sprint

The problem

Every ERP demo can look good.

Sales demos rarely expose implementation complexity, reporting gaps, integration work, data migration risk, or how the system will support real finance and operations workflows.

Selection should clarify:

  • Business and department requirements
  • Required implementation partner capabilities
  • Total cost and timeline expectations
  • Reporting and dashboard needs
  • Data migration and integration complexity
  • Risks that leadership should understand before signing

Selection process

Structured evaluation from requirements to recommendation.

Requirements

Interview finance, operations, sales, procurement, and leadership to define what ERP must support.

Shortlist

Build a realistic shortlist across NetSuite, Dynamics 365, Sage Intacct, Acumatica, Odoo, and industry systems.

RFP and demos

Prepare demo scripts and scorecards so vendors prove fit against your actual processes.

Decision support

Compare software, partner fit, cost, timeline, risks, and implementation readiness for executives.

Deliverables

Selection outputs leadership can use.

  • Requirements workbook
  • Vendor shortlist
  • RFP and demo script
  • ERP scorecard
  • Implementation partner review
  • Cost comparison
  • Final executive recommendation
