Problem-aware ERP consulting

ERP Problems We Help Solve

ERP issues usually show up as reporting delays, manual work, frustrated users, broken integrations, or month-end close pressure. We identify the root cause and create a practical path forward.

Your team still depends on spreadsheets

Your ERP should be the source of truth, but teams often still export data, clean spreadsheets, and reconcile numbers by hand.

Reports do not match across systems

Finance, sales, operations, and leadership may be looking at different numbers. We review the data flow so teams can trust the numbers.

Your ERP implementation is delayed

Projects slow down when requirements are unclear, data is messy, testing is weak, or ownership is missing.

Month-end close takes too long

If close depends on manual entries, offline reconciliations, delayed approvals, or disconnected systems, we redesign workflows and controls.

Integrations are breaking

CRM, billing, procurement, ecommerce, HR, banking, and reporting tools need clear mappings, error handling, and ownership.

Users do not trust the ERP

When users believe data is wrong or the process is confusing, adoption suffers. We improve workflows, training, reporting, and support.

Data migration feels risky

Customers, vendors, items, balances, open transactions, and history must be cleaned and validated before go-live.

Leadership lacks clear dashboards

Executives need reliable visibility into revenue, cash, inventory, margin, operations, and project performance.

You are unsure which ERP to choose

Vendor demos can make every system look good. We define requirements, compare platforms, score vendors, and clarify fit.

Our ERP advisory method

From confusion to clear decisions.

We use a structured, practical method to understand the business first, diagnose root causes, design the right roadmap, and support execution.

  1. 01

    Discover

    We review processes, systems, reports, integrations, pain points, and responsibilities.

  2. 02

    Diagnose

    We separate software problems from process, data, configuration, integration, training, and ownership problems.

  3. 03

    Design

    We define future-state workflows, requirements, reporting needs, controls, integrations, and priorities.

  4. 04

    Select or Improve

    We help select the right ERP and partner, or prioritize practical fixes in your current system.

  5. 05

    Implement

    We support planning, configuration review, data migration, testing, training, cutover, and readiness.

  6. 06

    Stabilize

    We help resolve issues, improve reporting, support users, and build the next optimization roadmap.

Migration readiness

Starting point
Duplicate records, unclear ownership, and reconciliation rules still being defined.
Engagement work
Data inventory, mapping decisions, cleanup ownership, test cycles, and reconciliation gates.
Decision outcome
A repeatable migration process with clear acceptance criteria before cutover.
