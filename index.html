Maitero lifecycle

Assess. Define. Select. Design. Implement. Test. Launch. Stabilize. Optimize.

The methodology keeps decisions, requirements, data, integrations, testing, and business ownership visible through the ERP lifecycle.

  1. 01

    Assess

    Review maturity, risks, systems, processes, reporting, and ownership.

  2. 02

    Define

    Document requirements, controls, data, integrations, and reporting needs.

  3. 03

    Select

    Compare platforms, partners, demos, costs, and implementation risk.

  4. 04

    Design

    Review configuration, workflows, roles, reports, and integration architecture.

  5. 05

    Test

    Trace requirements through UAT, defects, reports, and sign-off.

  6. 06

    Launch

    Confirm data, cutover, training, support, and go/no-go readiness.

  7. 07

    Stabilize

    Triage defects, reporting, support, and user adoption after launch.

  8. 08

    Optimize

    Prioritize improvements, automation, controls, and managed support.
