How Meesam ERP handles information submitted through this website.

Effective July 17, 2026. This notice applies to information collected through this website.

Information you provide

When you submit the contact form or send an email, Meesam ERP may receive your name, company, email address, phone number, current ERP, company size, project challenge, timeline, preferred meeting window, and message.

How information is used

Information is used to review your request, respond to you, prepare a proposed consultation or engagement, maintain business records, protect the website, and comply with applicable obligations. Meesam ERP does not sell personal information submitted through this site.

Form delivery

The contact form is delivered through FormSubmit, a third-party form processing service. Information submitted through the form is transmitted to that service and then delivered by email. Do not include passwords, financial account details, confidential datasets, protected health information, or other highly sensitive information in the form.

Retention and security

Inquiry information may be retained for as long as reasonably needed to respond, manage a potential or active business relationship, maintain records, resolve disputes, and meet legal obligations. Reasonable measures are used to protect information, but no internet transmission or email system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your choices

You may ask Meesam ERP to correct or delete inquiry information, subject to applicable recordkeeping needs. You may also choose to contact Meesam ERP directly by email instead of using the website form.
