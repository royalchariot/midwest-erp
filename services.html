How we work

We separate ERP symptoms from root causes.

A reporting issue may really be a data ownership issue. A delayed implementation may really be a requirements issue. A frustrated user team may be dealing with unclear workflows, weak training, or missing controls.

Every engagement looks at:

  • Finance and accounting workflows
  • Operational process requirements
  • Data quality and ownership
  • Integration flow and error handling
  • Reporting and leadership visibility
  • Testing, training, and go-live readiness

Quick ERP Answers

What does Maitero ERP do?

Maitero helps companies assess ERP readiness, select the right platform, support implementations, migrate data, connect systems, and improve reporting after go-live.

Which ERP platforms are supported?

Core advisory covers NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Sage Intacct, Acumatica, Odoo, QuickBooks transitions, and connected finance operations tools.

When should we contact Maitero?

Reach out before selection, during a stalled implementation, ahead of data migration, when integrations are disconnected, or when reporting is slowing decisions.

How fast is the first response?

Consultation requests are reviewed within one business day, with the first call focused on scope, risks, timeline, and the best next decision.
