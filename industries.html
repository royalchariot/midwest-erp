Industries

ERP Consulting for Operating Businesses With Real Process Complexity

Different industries have different ERP requirements. We translate your operational reality into clear system requirements and implementation priorities.

Manufacturing

Manufacturers need ERP systems that support inventory, purchasing, production planning, bills of materials, costing, quality, work orders, and financial reporting.

  • Inventory visibility
  • Production planning workflows
  • Purchasing process design
  • Costing and margin reporting
  • Shop floor and finance alignment

Wholesale Distribution

Distributors need reliable inventory, warehouse operations, pricing, purchasing, order fulfillment, returns, and margin visibility.

  • Warehouse process review
  • Inventory accuracy improvement
  • Order-to-cash workflow design
  • ERP and WMS integration planning

Professional Services

Services firms need ERP support for project accounting, resource planning, billing, expenses, revenue, utilization, and profitability.

  • Project billing workflows
  • Resource and utilization reporting
  • Expense and approval flows
  • Project profitability dashboards

Retail & Ecommerce

Retail and ecommerce companies need ERP connected with online orders, payments, inventory, fulfillment, returns, tax, and customer data.

  • Ecommerce to ERP integration
  • Payment reconciliation
  • Inventory and fulfillment visibility
  • Sales and margin reporting

Construction & Field Services

Construction and field service businesses need ERP support for job costing, purchasing, work orders, progress billing, assets, approvals, and project reporting.

  • Job cost structure
  • Purchase order workflows
  • Field service integration
  • Project financial reporting

Technology & SaaS

SaaS and technology companies need scalable ERP processes for CRM-to-ERP flow, subscription billing, revenue recognition, payments, reporting, and investor-ready finance operations.

  • Salesforce to ERP process design
  • Subscription billing workflows
  • Revenue recognition support
  • SaaS KPI reporting
  • Close process improvement
