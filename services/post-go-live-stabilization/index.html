Stabilize and Rescue

Service summary

Hypercare, defect prioritization, reporting stabilization, data remediation, integration monitoring, and user support after launch.

Typical timeline: 30, 60, or 90 day stabilization plans.Primary CTA: Plan Post-Go-Live Stabilization

Who this service is for

  • Teams recently live on ERP
  • Finance teams dealing with launch defects
  • Executives who need reliable reporting after go-live

Problems addressed

  • Users are confused
  • Reports do not reconcile
  • Integrations fail
  • Defects pile up
  • Month-end close is unstable

Warning signs

  • Teams rebuild reports in spreadsheets
  • Support tickets repeat
  • No one owns enhancement priorities

Questions Maitero answers

  • What should be fixed first?
  • Which issues are defects versus enhancements?
  • How should support be structured?
  • What does stable operations require?
