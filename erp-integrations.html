Integration problems

Disconnected systems create bad data and manual work.

When integrations are unclear, teams compensate with exports, uploads, duplicate entry, and reconciliation work that slows close and weakens trust.

Common integration areas

  • CRM to ERP
  • Salesforce to NetSuite or Dynamics
  • Ecommerce to ERP
  • Procurement to ERP
  • Stripe and payment processors
  • Payroll and HRIS
  • Banking, SFTP, EDI, and API flows
  • Data warehouse and BI tools
